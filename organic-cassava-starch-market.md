<p><p>The Organic Cassava Starch market encompasses two primary types: Modified Cassava Starch and Native Cassava Starch. Modified Cassava Starch is chemically or physically altered to enhance its properties, making it suitable for various applications in food production, pharmaceuticals, and industrial uses.
