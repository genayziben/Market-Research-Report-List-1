<p><p>The metal fabrication market encompasses various processes essential for shaping and assembling metal components. Metal welding involves joining metal parts using heat, while metal machining refers to removing material to achieve desired shapes. Metal forming reshapes materials through techniques like bending and stretching. Metal shearing cuts metal sheets, and metal cutting precisely slices materials into specified sizes. Metal folding involves bending metal sheets, metal rolling creates curved shapes, and metal stamping uses dies to produce complex parts, while metal punching creates holes in materials.</p></p>
