<p><p>Optical disc storage technology encompasses various market types, including Read-Only, Write-Once, Erasable Rewritable, and Photo Discs. Read-Only discs, like CDs and DVDs, are pre-pressed and cannot be written over. Write-Once discs allow users to record data a single time, making them ideal for archival purposes. Erasable Rewritable discs can be erased and rewritten multiple times, offering flexibility for data management. Photo Discs, specialized for storing images, combine high-quality formats with storage efficiency, catering to consumers and professionals alike.</p></p>
