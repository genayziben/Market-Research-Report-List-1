<p><p>The power tool battery market features several battery types, each serving distinct needs. Lithium-Ion batteries are preferred for their high energy density, lightweight design, and longer lifespan, making them ideal for modern power tools. Ni-Cad batteries offer reliable performance and quick charging but have a lower energy density and suffer from memory effect. NiMH batteries provide a middle ground with better capacity than Ni-Cad and less environmental impact. Other battery types may include lead-acid and emerging technologies, catering to specialized applications.</p></p>
