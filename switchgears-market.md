<p><p>The switchgear market is segmented into low, medium, and high voltage categories. Low voltage electrical switchgear handles voltages up to 1 kV, primarily used in residential and commercial applications. Medium voltage switching equipment operates between 1 kV and 36 kV, suitable for industrial and utility systems. High voltage electronic switchgear, exceeding 36 kV, is essential for power transmission and distribution infrastructure. Each type serves distinct functions in controlling, protecting, and isolating electrical equipment to ensure safe and reliable operation.</p></p>
