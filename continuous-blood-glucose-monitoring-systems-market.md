<p><p>The Continuous Blood Glucose Monitoring Systems market comprises two primary types: Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose Systems (SMBG) and Continuous Glucose Monitoring Systems (CGMS). SMBG involves patients using portable devices to check their blood glucose levels at specific intervals, providing insights for day-to-day management. In contrast, CGMS offers real-time, continuous tracking of glucose levels through sensors placed under the skin, allowing for dynamic monitoring and better management of diabetes. Both systems aim to enhance patient outcomes through improved glucose control.</p></p>
