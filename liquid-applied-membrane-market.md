<p><ul><li>Bituminous Membrane</li><li>Elastomeric Membrane</li><li>Cementitious Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquid Applied Membrane Market includes three primary types: Bituminous Membrane, Elastomeric Membrane, and Cementitious Membrane. Bituminous Membranes are made from asphalt and are ideal for waterproofing roofs and foundations. Elastomeric Membranes, known for their flexibility and durability, are used in various applications, including buildings and bridges, as they can withstand temperature changes. Cementitious Membranes, composed of cement-based materials, provide excellent waterproofing and are often used in concrete structures, offering strong adhesion and durability.</p></p>
