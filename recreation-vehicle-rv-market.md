<p><p>The Recreation Vehicle (RV) market comprises two main categories: motorized RVs and towable RVs. Motorized RVs are self-propelled vehicles, including Class A, B, and C models, offering built-in living spaces for travelers. Towable RVs, on the other hand, require a separate vehicle for transportation and include travel trailers, fifth wheels, and pop-up campers. Both types cater to various consumer preferences, providing flexibility, comfort, and adventure for outdoor enthusiasts and travelers seeking mobile living experiences.</p></p>
