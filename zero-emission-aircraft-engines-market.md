<p><p>The zero-emission aircraft engines market encompasses three main types: hydrogen engines, electric engines, and solar engines. Hydrogen engines utilize fuel cells to convert hydrogen into electricity, producing only water vapor as a byproduct. Electric engines rely on rechargeable batteries to power electric motors, offering silent and efficient operation. Solar engines harness solar energy through photovoltaic cells to generate power for propulsion. Together, these technologies aim to reduce aviation's carbon footprint, promoting sustainable air travel and addressing climate concerns.</p></p>
