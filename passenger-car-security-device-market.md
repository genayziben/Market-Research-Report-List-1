<p><p>The Passenger Car Security Device market encompasses several key product types: immobilizers, vehicle alarms, central locking systems, and remote keyless entry (RKE) systems. Immobilizers prevent engine start unless the correct key or fob is detected, deterring theft. Vehicle alarms enhance security with sound alerts to unauthorized access. Central locking systems enable simultaneous locking or unlocking of all doors for convenience. RKE systems allow users to control door locks from a distance, offering added ease and protection in modern vehicles.</p></p>
