<p><ul><li>NTC Thermistors</li><li>Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs)</li><li>Thermocouples</li><li>Semiconductor-Based Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless temperature sensors for remote monitoring encompass various types including NTC thermistors, resistance temperature detectors (RTDs), thermocouples, and semiconductor-based sensors. NTC thermistors are temperature-sensitive resistors that decrease resistance with rising temperature. RTDs offer high accuracy and stability, providing resistance changes based on temperature variations. Thermocouples generate a voltage based on temperature differences between two junctions, suitable for extreme conditions. Semiconductor-based sensors utilize materials that change electrical properties with temperature, allowing for compact and efficient designs. These technologies enable effective temperature monitoring across diverse applications.</p></p>
