<p><p>The static precision toolholder market comprises various types, primarily focusing on BMT (Bolt Type) and VDI (Verein Deutscher Ingenieure) systems. BMT toolholders are designed for quick tool changes, enhancing efficiency and reducing downtime in machining processes. In contrast, VDI toolholders offer a standardized interface, allowing for high precision and versatility across different machines. Both types cater to the demand for increased productivity and accuracy in manufacturing, supporting a range of applications in precision machining and industrial automation.</p></p>
