<p><strong>The Gram Stain Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gram's Crystal Violet Solution</li><li>Gram's Iodine Solution</li><li>Gram's Decolorizer Solution</li><li>Gram's Safranin Solution</li><li>Gram's Tartrazine Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gram stain kits market comprises several specialized solutions essential for differentiating bacterial species. Gram's Crystal Violet Solution initially stains all bacteria, while Gram's Iodine Solution acts as a mordant, fixing the violet dye. The Gram's Decolorizer Solution differentiates between Gram-positive and Gram-negative bacteria by removing the dye from certain types. Lastly, Gram's Safranin Solution provides a counterstain, coloring decolorized bacteria, while Gram's Tartrazine Solution is used for visual enhancement in some applications. Each solution plays a critical role in microbial analysis.</p></p>
