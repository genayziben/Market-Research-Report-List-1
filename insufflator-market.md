<p><p>The insufflator market is categorized into high flow, medium flow, and low flow insufflators, each serving specific clinical needs. High flow insufflators deliver gas at rapid rates, ideal for procedures requiring quick abdominal distension. Medium flow insufflators provide a balanced option for routine surgeries, ensuring adequate distension without excessive pressure. Low flow insufflators are designed for minimally invasive procedures where controlled inflation is necessary, promoting ventilation while minimizing patient discomfort. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing surgical efficiency and safety.</p></p>
