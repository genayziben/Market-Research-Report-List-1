<p><p>The semiconductor wafer etching agents market is primarily divided into two types: wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching agents use liquid solutions to remove material from the wafer surface, offering uniform etching and simplicity in real-time monitoring. In contrast, dry etching agents employ gases or plasma, allowing for precise control and the ability to create intricate patterns with minimal undercutting. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor fabrication, impacting performance and efficiency in chip manufacturing processes.</p></p>
