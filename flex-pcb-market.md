Multi-layer circuits consist of multiple layers, providing complex interconnections suitable for advanced electronics. Rigid-flex circuits combine rigid and flexible substrates, enabling intricate designs with enhanced durability, often used in compact spaces. Each type serves distinct needs, driving innovation across consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
