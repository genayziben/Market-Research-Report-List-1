<p><p>The mineral wool market is primarily classified into two main types: glass wool and stone wool. Glass wool is made from melted glass fibers and is renowned for its excellent thermal insulation and soundproofing properties, making it ideal for residential and commercial applications. Stone wool, derived from volcanic rock or basalt, offers superior fire resistance and durability, often used in industrial and high-heat environments. Both types serve critical roles in construction, HVAC systems, and energy efficiency initiatives.</p></p>
