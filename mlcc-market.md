<p><strong>The MLCC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X7R</li><li>X5R</li><li>C0G (NP0)</li><li>Y5V</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MLCCs (Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors) are categorized based on their dielectric properties. X7R features wide temperature stability and moderate capacitance, making it suitable for general applications. X5R offers similar benefits but with lower capacitance variation, ideal for decoupling. C0G (NP0) provides exceptional stability and is used in precision applications, while Y5V has a significant capacitance drop under voltage and temperature, suited for less demanding tasks. Other types may include less common dielectrics tailored for specific applications or industries.</p></p>
