<p><p>The pressure relief regulator valve market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Diaphragm Type**: Utilizes a flexible diaphragm to maintain pressure control, ideal for low to moderate flows.</p><p>2. **Spring Film Type**: Employs a spring-loaded mechanism, offering quick response for high-pressure applications.</p><p>3. **Piston Type**: Features a piston for precise control, suitable for high fluid velocities.</p><p>4. **Leveraged Type**: Uses levers for increased force, enhancing pressure regulation.</p><p>5. **Bellows Type**: Incorporates bellows for excellent sealing and adaptability in varying pressure conditions. </p><p>These types cater to diverse industrial applications, ensuring efficient pressure management.</p></p>
