<p><p>The steel powder market is primarily categorized into several production types: atomization, reduction, and others. Atomization involves transforming molten steel into fine particles through high-pressure gas or water jets, ensuring uniformity and high purity. Reduction, on the other hand, uses chemical processes to convert iron ore into a powdered form, which is often cheaper but may offer lower purity. Other methods include mechanical milling and electrolysis, catering to specific industrial applications and quality requirements in various sectors.</p></p>
