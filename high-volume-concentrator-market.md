Two key types within this market are zeolite and activated carbon. Zeolites are crystalline aluminosilicates known for their ion-exchange properties, making them ideal for moisture removal and gas separation. Activated carbon, with its extensive surface area, excels in adsorbing volatile organic compounds and pollutants. Both materials play crucial roles in environmental remediation, air purification, and industrial processes, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
