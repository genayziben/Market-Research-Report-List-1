<p><p>The small wind turbine market is primarily divided into two types: horizontal-axis wind turbines (HAWT) and vertical-axis wind turbines (VAWT). HAWTs feature blades that rotate around a horizontal axis, offering higher efficiency and greater energy capture, making them ideal for commercial use. Conversely, VAWTs have blades that rotate around a vertical axis, allowing them to harness wind from any direction, making them suitable for urban settings and areas with variable wind patterns. Both types cater to diverse energy needs and environments.</p></p>
