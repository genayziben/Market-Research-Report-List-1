<p><strong>The Photosensitive Polymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photosensitive Reversible</li><li>Photosensitive Irreversible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photosensitive polymer market is divided into two main types: photosensitive reversible and photosensitive irreversible polymers. Photosensitive reversible polymers undergo a reversible chemical change when exposed to light, allowing them to be reshaped or reused, making them suitable for applications such as 3D printing and lithography. On the other hand, photosensitive irreversible polymers undergo a permanent change upon light exposure, resulting in a fixed structure, which is ideal for use in coatings, adhesives, and microfabrication processes.</p></p>
