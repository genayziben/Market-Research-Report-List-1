<p><p>The aluminum curtain wall system market consists of two primary types: unitized and stick-built systems. Unitized systems involve prefabricated panels that are assembled in a factory and installed as complete units, offering speed and efficiency in construction. In contrast, stick-built systems are constructed on-site, using individual components assembled to form the wall. While stick-built systems provide flexibility and design adaptability, unitized systems are favored for their quick installation and reduced labor costs, making both types suitable for various architectural applications.</p></p>
