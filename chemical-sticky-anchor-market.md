<p><strong>The Chemical Sticky Anchor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injectable Adhensive Anchors</li><li>Capsule Adhensive Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemical Sticky Anchor market comprises primarily two types: Injectable Adhesive Anchors and Capsule Adhesive Anchors. Injectable Adhesive Anchors are versatile systems that allow for the direct application of adhesive into the drilled hole, providing strong bonding with various substrates. In contrast, Capsule Adhesive Anchors come in pre-packaged capsules that release adhesive upon installation, simplifying the process and ensuring consistent mixing. Both types are widely used in construction and civil engineering for anchoring applications, offering robust solutions for load-bearing needs.</p></p>
