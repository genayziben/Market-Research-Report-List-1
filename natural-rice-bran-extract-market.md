<p><p>The Natural Rice Bran Extract market is categorized into two main types: Extraction Extract and Squeezing Extract. Extraction Extract involves using solvents or natural processes to separate beneficial compounds from rice bran, enhancing nutrient concentration and potency. In contrast, Squeezing Extract utilizes mechanical pressure to directly extract oils and nutrients from the bran without additional solvents, preserving the integrity of the components. Both methods cater to diverse applications in food, cosmetics, and nutraceutical industries, emphasizing quality and efficacy.</p></p>
