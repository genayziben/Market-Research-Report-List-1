<p><p>The mechatronic lock market can be categorized into two main types: contact type and contactless type. Contact type locks require a physical connection, typically involving a mechanical key or card, to engage the locking mechanism. In contrast, contactless type locks utilize technologies such as RFID, Bluetooth, or biometrics, allowing users to unlock doors without direct contact. This differentiation highlights the broad range of applications, from traditional security needs to modern, tech-driven access solutions that prioritize convenience and enhanced security features.</p></p>
