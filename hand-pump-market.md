<p><strong>The Hand Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Pump</li><li>Lever Action Pump</li><li>Piston Pump</li><li>Drum Siphon Pump</li><li>Hydraulic Hand Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hand pump market features various types, each suited for different applications. Rotary pumps utilize a rotating mechanism for fluid transfer, ideal for continuous flow. Lever action pumps leverage a handle for manual operation, typically used for smaller volumes. Piston pumps draw fluid through a cylinder mechanism, effective for high-pressure applications. Drum siphon pumps facilitate liquid extraction from barrels using gravity. Hydraulic hand pumps generate hydraulic pressure for equipment, essential in tasks requiring forceful lifting or moving.</p></p>
