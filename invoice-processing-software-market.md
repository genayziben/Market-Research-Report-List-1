<p><p>Invoice processing software can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on company servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on the vendor's servers, providing accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection, automatic updates, and lower initial costs. Businesses choose between these types based on their IT resources, budget, and need for scalability or flexibility.</p></p>
