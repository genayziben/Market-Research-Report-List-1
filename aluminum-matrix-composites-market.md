<p><p>Aluminum Matrix Composites (AMCs) are categorized mainly into two types: Particle Enhanced and Fiber Enhanced. </p><p>Particle Enhanced types involve the incorporation of hard particles, such as ceramics, which improve strength, wear resistance, and thermal stability. </p><p>On the other hand, Fiber Enhanced types utilize fibers, like carbon or silicon, to provide enhanced tensile strength and stiffness, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including aerospace, automotive, and electronics, enhancing material properties for specific applications.</p></p>
