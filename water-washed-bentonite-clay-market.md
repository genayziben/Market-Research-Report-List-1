<p><p>The water-washed bentonite clay market is segmented into three main types: sodium bentonite, calcium bentonite, and potassium bentonite. Sodium bentonite is known for its high swelling capacity and is widely used in drilling fluids and sealants. Calcium bentonite is preferred in applications such as foundry sands and agricultural enhancement due to its lower swelling and more stable properties. Potassium bentonite, often utilized for its binding properties, is primarily found in specialty products, including fertilizers and cosmetics.</p></p>
