<p><strong>The Drone Transponders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ADS-B Compatible</li><li>ADS-B Non-Compatible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drone transponders market is categorized into two main types: ADS-B compatible and ADS-B non-compatible. ADS-B compatible transponders are integrated with Automatic Dependent Surveillance–Broadcast systems, enabling real-time tracking and communication with air traffic control and other aircraft, enhancing safety and regulatory compliance. Conversely, ADS-B non-compatible transponders lack this functionality, which may limit their operational range and interoperability in controlled airspace. As regulations evolve, the preference for ADS-B compatible systems is likely to increase for broader operational capabilities.</p></p>
