<p><p>The Manual-Tie Balers market is primarily categorized into two types: vertical and horizontal balers. Vertical balers are compact and efficient for small spaces, using gravity to facilitate the baling process and often suited for lighter materials. In contrast, horizontal balers are larger and more powerful, designed for higher volume operations, effectively compressing heavier materials. Both types require manual tying of the bales, catering to different operational scales and material handling needs in recycling and waste management industries.</p></p>
