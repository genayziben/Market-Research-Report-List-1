<p><p>Ecotoxicological studies encompass various market types that assess the environmental impact of pollutants on distinct ecosystems. Aquatic ecotoxicology focuses on the effects of chemicals in water bodies, while sediment ecotoxicology deals with pollutants in sediment layers. Terrestrial ecotoxicology examines soil and land environments, assessing the risks to plants and animals. Avian ecotoxicology targets the effects on bird species, and pollinator testing evaluates chemical impacts on species like bees, essential for ecosystem health and food production.</p></p>
