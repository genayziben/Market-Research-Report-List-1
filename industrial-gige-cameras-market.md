<p><strong>The Industrial GigE Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Cameras</li><li>Line Scan Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial GigE Camera market is primarily divided into two types: Area Scan Cameras and Line Scan Cameras. Area Scan Cameras capture 2D images of a scene in full, ideal for stationary objects and applications like quality inspection. In contrast, Line Scan Cameras capture images one line at a time, making them suitable for continuous motion applications, such as conveyor belts in manufacturing. Both types leverage GigE interfaces for high-speed data transfer, enhancing performance in industrial automation and imaging tasks.</p></p>
