<p><strong>The Crystalline Waterproofing Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystalline Waterproofing Coating</li><li>Crystalline Waterproofing Admixtures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Crystalline Waterproofing Material market is primarily divided into two types: Crystalline Waterproofing Coatings and Crystalline Waterproofing Admixtures. Crystalline Waterproofing Coatings are applied as a surface layer, forming crystals that fill pores and micro-cracks in concrete, creating a waterproof barrier. In contrast, Crystalline Waterproofing Admixtures are integrated into the concrete mix itself, enhancing the material's inherent waterproofing properties. Both types provide effective protection against water infiltration, extending the lifespan of structures while maintaining their structural integrity.</p></p>
