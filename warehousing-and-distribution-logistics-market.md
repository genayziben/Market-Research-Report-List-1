It encompasses strategies for optimizing space, managing stock levels, and implementing automated systems. Distribution logistics, on the other hand, involves the planning and execution of transporting goods from warehouses to end consumers, ensuring timely delivery, route optimization, and cost-effectiveness. Together, these two market types enhance supply chain efficiency and customer satisfaction.</p></p>
