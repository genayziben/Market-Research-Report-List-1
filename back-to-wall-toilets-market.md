<p><p>The back to wall toilets market comprises two main types: single flush toilets and dual flush toilets. Single flush toilets utilize one flush mechanism for all waste types, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, dual flush toilets provide two flush options—typically a lower volume for liquid waste and a higher volume for solid waste—promoting water conservation. Both types cater to consumer preferences for functionality, design, and environmental concerns, influencing trends in modern bathroom fixtures.</p></p>
