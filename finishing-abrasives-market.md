<p><ul><li>Bonded</li><li>Coated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Finishing Abrasives Market includes **Bonded** and **Coated** types. Bonded abrasives, like grinding wheels and stones, are made by bonding abrasive grains with resins, ceramics, or vitrified materials, offering precision in shaping and finishing. Coated abrasives, such as sandpaper or belts, feature abrasive grains adhered to a flexible backing, ideal for surface smoothing and polishing. Both types cater to industries like automotive, metalworking, and woodworking, providing tailored solutions for material removal, finishing, and surface refinement.</p></p>
