<p><strong>The Touch Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GF2</li><li>GFF</li><li>GG DITO</li><li>GG or SITO</li><li>OGS/G2</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch panel market features several types based on construction and touch technology. GF2 (Glass-Film-Film) uses a glass layer and two film layers, offering durability. GFF (Glass-Film-Film) consists of a glass and two film layers for enhanced flexibility. GG or SITO (Glass-Glass or Sensor-In- Touch) incorporates two glass layers, providing superior sensitivity. OGS (One Glass Solution) combines touch and display layers into one glass unit, streamlining design and reducing thickness, while G2 (Glass-Glass) denotes a simpler, robust glass configuration.</p></p>
