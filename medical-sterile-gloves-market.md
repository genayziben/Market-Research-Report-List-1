<p><p>The medical sterile gloves market is segmented into four main types: latex, vinyl, nitrile rubber, and neoprene. Latex gloves offer excellent elasticity and comfort but can cause allergic reactions in some users. Vinyl gloves are cost-effective and suitable for low-risk tasks, though less durable. Nitrile rubber gloves provide superior puncture resistance and are widely used in environments where latex allergies are a concern. Neoprene gloves combine flexibility and chemical resistance, making them ideal for various medical applications.</p></p>
