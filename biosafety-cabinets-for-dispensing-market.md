<p><ul><li>Class II A Type</li><li>Class II B Type</li><li>Class III Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biosafety cabinets (BSCs) for dispensing are essential for ensuring safety in laboratories handling hazardous materials. Class II A Type BSCs provide personnel protection while allowing some air recirculation. Class II B Type BSCs offer enhanced protection with vertical airflow and no recirculation, ideal for more hazardous activities. Class III Type BSCs are the highest level, providing a completely sealed environment with gas-tight construction, suitable for maximum containment of highly infectious agents. Each type serves specific safety and procedural needs in laboratory environments.</p></p>
