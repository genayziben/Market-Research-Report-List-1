<p><p>The mechanical keyboard switches market can be categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Keyclick**: Produces an audible click sound and tactile feedback, popular among typists for its satisfying response.</p><p>2. **Linear Action**: Offers a smooth keystroke with no tactile bump or noise, ideal for gamers seeking rapid keypresses.</p><p>3. **Soft Contact**: Features a soft actuation, minimizing force needed, perfect for users who prefer a gentle typing experience.</p><p>4. **Alternate Action**: Designed for dual functions, often used in applications like gaming controllers.</p><p>5. **Ergonomic**: Focuses on comfort, reducing strain during extended use with contoured designs and optimized key travel.</p></p>
