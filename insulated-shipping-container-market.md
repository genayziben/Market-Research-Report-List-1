<p><p>The insulated shipping container market comprises primarily two types: Non-Operational (NOR) refrigerated containers and insulated purpose-built shipping containers. NOR refrigerated containers are designed for transporting temperature-sensitive cargo without active cooling; they rely on insulation to maintain temperature. In contrast, insulated purpose-built containers are specifically engineered with thermal insulation for various goods, enhancing their protection during transit. Both types are essential for industries requiring climate control, ensuring the safe delivery of perishable and sensitive products globally.</p></p>
