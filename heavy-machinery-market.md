<p><p>The heavy machinery market comprises three main types: complete vehicles, incomplete vehicles, and semitrailer vehicles. Complete vehicles are fully functional machines ready for use, such as excavators and bulldozers. Incomplete vehicles are chassis or frames that require additional components, often customized for specific tasks. Semitrailer vehicles are designed to haul trailers, providing versatility in transporting heavy loads. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, catering to construction, mining, and transportation sectors, thus driving demand and innovation in the machinery market.</p></p>
