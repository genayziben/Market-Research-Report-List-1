<p><p>The HVAC chillers market consists of three primary types: screw chillers, scroll chillers, and centrifugal chillers. Screw chillers use twin rotors for efficient cooling, ideal for medium to large applications with varying loads. Scroll chillers feature a compact design and are suitable for smaller spaces, offering high efficiency at lower capacities. Centrifugal chillers operate at high capacities, utilizing centrifugal force, making them ideal for large commercial or industrial applications. Each type serves specific cooling needs in diverse environments.</p></p>
