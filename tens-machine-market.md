<p><strong>The TENS Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TENS (Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation) machine market can be categorized into single-channel and dual-channel devices. Single-channel TENS machines deliver electrical impulses through one electrode pair, suitable for targeting localized pain areas. In contrast, dual-channel devices can operate two electrode pairs simultaneously, allowing for broader pain relief across multiple areas or varied intensities. Dual-channel machines often cater to more complex pain management needs, making them popular among users seeking versatile treatment options for diverse conditions.</p></p>
