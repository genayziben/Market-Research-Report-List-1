<p><p>Accumulators are essential components in hydraulic systems, serving to store energy and maintain pressure. The Weight-Loaded Piston Type uses gravitational force for energy storage, while the Diaphragm (Bladder) Type employs a flexible membrane to separate fluids and compress gas for pressure stabilization. The Spring Type relies on mechanical springs to store energy, and the Hydro-Pneumatic Piston Type combines liquid and gas to achieve energy storage and pressure regulation. Each type addresses specific application needs in various industrial settings.</p></p>
