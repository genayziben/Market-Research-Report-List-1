<p><p>The gastrointestinal endoscopic device market comprises primarily two types: flexible endoscopes and capsule endoscopes. Flexible endoscopes are long, flexible tubes equipped with a camera and light source, allowing doctors to directly visualize and diagnose conditions within the digestive tract. Capsule endoscopes, on the other hand, are small, swallowable capsules with a built-in camera that capture images as they travel through the gastrointestinal tract, offering a non-invasive alternative for examining areas that are difficult to reach with traditional endoscopes.</p></p>
