<p><p>The Organic Fluorescent Pigment market comprises various types, including Thermoset and Thermoplastic categories. Thermoset pigments, once cured, retain their shape and stability under heat, making them suitable for durable applications. In contrast, Thermoplastic pigments remain flexible and can be remolded, ideal for dynamic applications. Additionally, there are Aqueous Dispersions, which are water-based, offering environmentally friendly options for coating and printing applications. Each type serves distinct needs in industries such as plastics, textiles, and paints.</p></p>
