<p><strong>The Epoxy Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy adhesive market is segmented into one-component and two-component types. One-component epoxies are pre-mixed, ready-to-use adhesives that cure with heat or moisture, offering convenience and ease of application. They are ideal for industries requiring quick, single-step bonding. Two-component epoxies consist of a resin and a hardener, mixed before use, providing superior strength, durability, and customization for demanding applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, with one-component favoring simplicity and two-component excelling in high-performance needs.</p></p>
