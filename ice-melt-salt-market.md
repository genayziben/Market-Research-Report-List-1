<p><p>The ice melt salt market is categorized into two main types: liquid and solid ice melt salts. Liquid ice melt salts consist of various chemicals formulated for rapid application, providing quick de-icing on surfaces. They are often used in extreme weather conditions where immediate melting is necessary. Solid ice melt salts, on the other hand, include granulated products like sodium chloride or calcium chloride, which release heat when they dissolve, gradually melting ice over time and providing longer-lasting effects.</p></p>
