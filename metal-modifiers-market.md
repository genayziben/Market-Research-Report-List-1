<p><p>The metal modifiers market is divided into organic and inorganic types. Organic metal modifiers are typically carbon-based compounds that improve properties like fluidity, strength, and adhesion in polymers and coatings. They often enhance performance and durability in various applications. In contrast, inorganic metal modifiers consist of metal oxides or salts that offer benefits such as thermal stability, corrosion resistance, and improved mechanical properties. Both types are essential in industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction for enhancing material performance.</p></p>
