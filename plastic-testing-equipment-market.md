<p><p>The plastic testing equipment market includes various types designed to evaluate the properties of plastics. The Universal Testing Machine measures tensile strength, compression, and elongation. A Capillary Rheometer assesses flow behavior and viscosity during processing. The Pendulum Tester evaluates impact resistance by simulating stress conditions. The Melt Flow Tester gauges the melt viscosity and flow rate of thermoplastics at high temperatures, helping manufacturers ensure product quality and performance. Each type plays a crucial role in quality control and material characterization.</p></p>
