<p><ul><li>Singlemode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The buoyant cable market primarily includes two types: singlemode and multimode cables. Singlemode cables utilize a single light pathway, allowing for long-distance transmission with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and high-speed networks. Multimode cables, on the other hand, support multiple light pathways, facilitating shorter-distance transmissions. They are typically used in local area networks and data centers. Both types cater to diverse applications, balancing performance needs with cost-effectiveness in buoyant cable solutions.</p></p>
