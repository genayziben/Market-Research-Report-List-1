<p><p>A wheel excavator is a versatile piece of construction equipment designed for various tasks, including digging, loading, and grading. It features a rotating upper structure mounted on a wheeled chassis, allowing for enhanced maneuverability and efficiency compared to traditional crawler excavators. These machines are often used in urban construction, infrastructure projects, and utility work, where space is limited and mobility is essential.</p><p>The Wheel Excavator Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period.
